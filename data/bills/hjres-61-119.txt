                            JOINT RESOLUTION


 
  Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, 
    United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental 
    Protection Agency relating to ``National Emission Standards for 
         Hazardous Air Pollutants: Rubber Tire Manufacturing''.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled,


              

 That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental 
Protection Agency relating to ``National Emission Standards for 
Hazardous Air Pollutants: Rubber Tire Manufacturing'' (89 Fed. Reg. 
94886 (November 29, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or 
effect.

            Passed the House of Representatives March 5, 2025.

            Attest:
